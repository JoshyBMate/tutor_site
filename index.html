About Us

Zoë Bell & Associates was founded by Zoë Kayla Bell, a passionate and highly qualified Remedial and Learning Support Therapist. Zoë has a Bachelor of Education degree (Cum Laude) as well as a Bachelor of Education Honours degree - Learning Support (Cum Laude) and is currently pursuing a Counselling Psychology degree.

Zoë Bell & Associates aims to provide holistic, quality and professional educational support in a therapeutic, loving and relaxing environment which caters for multiple learning needs and barriers to learning on all levels. We provide on-level and active learning and therapy in various fields, with the children’s best interests at the centre of it all.

All facilitation occurs on a personal level where the learner’s individual level of understanding is built upon, maximising understanding, building self-confidence and opening the doors for all to feel capable and love learning.
